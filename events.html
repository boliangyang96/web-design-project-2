Apple Harvest Open Air Farmers Market

Monday, Sept. 28 - Friday, Oct. 2 | 12:00pm - 4:00pm

Participating Vendors:

  • Robbies Produce Farm Market
  • AJ Teeter Farm
  • Littletree Orchards
  • Baked Up & Bubbled Over
  • Bakers Acres
  • Creamcycle

Be Safe & Socially Distant & Responsible

During the day, a couple of farms will have pop-up farm stands each afternoon on the commons to sell pre-packed Apples, Cider and other fall produce for contactless grab & go.

Lines with 6’ or more of social distance between customers will be in place at the farm stands and all customers and farmers are required to wear masks.

Hand sanitizer is stationed around the Ithaca Commons, and patrons are encouraged to bring their own for personal use as well.

Source: Downtown Ithaca Alliance

Apple & Cider Festive Trail

Monday, Sept. 28 - Sunday, Oct. 4 | 12:00pm - 4:00pm

Your passport to pomology!

Celebrate the apple season this year. Enjoy a little bit of the season each day with your quaranteam crew on a socially distant, contactless, taste-tastic journey of sipping, noshing, & shopping.

Apple & Cider Trail now available! Simply download the map, print & make your plans and let the fruitful week get started. Snap a photo of your experiences throughout the week and share them with us via Instagram or Facebook. Tag us @downtownithaca and hashtag it #FeelingAppleFestive to enter to win. No purchase necessary to participate.

Happening all week long inside shops and restaurants around downtown from Monday, Sept. 28 - Sunday, Oct. 4th. Enjoy ciders by the glass with your meal, juice for purchase, fun apple-inspired novelties, and even sweet and savory dishes showcasing the mighty fruit!

Guidelines how to shop in store will vary from in-store to online shopping. Curbside pick up options may also be available with participating businesses.

Finger Lakes Cider Week is also returning to the region October 2 - 11, 2020.

As Apple Festive winds down, Cider Week will ramp up with virtual tasting events online as well as Orchard tours and ticketed, socially distant food & cider pairing dinners.
